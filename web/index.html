About KiddoTubes

🎬 What is KiddoTubes?

KiddoTubes is a safe, kid-friendly video platform designed to provide age-appropriate entertainment and educational content for children aged 2-12 years.

🎯 Our Mission

To create a secure digital environment where children can explore, learn, and have fun while parents maintain full control and visibility.

🔒 Safety First

All content is carefully curated and filtered. Safe Search is enabled by default. No external links or inappropriate ads are allowed.

👨‍👩‍👧 Family-Focused

Parents have complete control over content, viewing time, and child settings. We believe in transparency and put families first.

Community Guidelines

KiddoTubes is for children — content must be age-appropriate, non-violent, non-sexual, and free from hate speech.

✅ What's Allowed

  • Educational and learning content
  • Age-appropriate children's cartoons
  • Music and songs for kids
  • Nursery rhymes and bedtime stories
  • Wholesome entertainment

🚫 Not Allowed

  • Violent or scary content
  • Adult language and profanity
  • Sexual or inappropriate content
  • Bullying or harassment
  • Personal information of children (names, addresses, schools)
  • Hate speech or discrimination
  • Misleading or harmful information

⚠️ Moderation

Uploads may be reviewed by human moderators and automated filters. Parents can flag content. Violations result in removal, warning, or suspension. Use the "Report" button to flag inappropriate content.
